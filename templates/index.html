#UNIcode

Instructions

To begin, type in the hashtag your tutor is tweeting questions on and press enter.

To answer a question, figure out what the expression evaluates to and type your answer in the respones box, then press enter.

Your progress will be recorded and you can tweet about it using the button below.

About

UNIcode is a Twitter-integrated tool you can use to learn code and syntax. Simply enter the hashtag that your tutor is tweeting questions with and start learning.
